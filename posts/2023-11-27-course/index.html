Introductory course to HLS FPGA programming

Nov 27, 2023 · 1 min read

I have been invited as Lecturer at the Introductory course to HLS FPGA programming, a course thought for the new personnel hired by means of the ICSC project to work on projects and research activities in the framework of Spoke 2 (“Fundamental Research And Space Economy”). It is a 3 days course, where I will give a lecture on the last day about the BondMachine project, and how it can be used to accelerate machine learning applications. It has been a pleasure to be invited to this course, and an occasion to build new documentation and hands-on material for the BondMachine project.
