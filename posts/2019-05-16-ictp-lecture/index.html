Lecture at ICTP

May 16, 2019 · 1 min read

I have been invited to ICTP to present the BondMachine project in the program of the Advanced Workshop on Modern FPGA Based Technology for Scientific Computing - The lecture was about the BondMachine project and contains a full hands-on session made on Zedboards. The Abdus Salam center is a place with an amazing atmosphere, where you can feel science and culture in the air. It have been a great honor beeing there, many thanks to Andres and Maria Liz for making this possible.
