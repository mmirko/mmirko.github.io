Home

My name is Mirko.
I am a System Engineer based in Perugia (Italy).
 
I was born in Perugia, Italy, in 1975. I received a B.Sc degree in Physics from the University of Perugia, Italy. Since 2002, I have been working as a System Engineer at the Department of Physics and Geology of the University of Perugia, where I am responsible for the department's ICT infrastructure. Additionally, I collaborate on software development for experiments and engage in ICT/Computer Science research and development.
 
My interests are multidisciplinary and range from Theoretical Physics and Foundations of Mathematics to Computational Science, High-Performance Computing (HPC), Evolutionary Algorithms, and Computer Architectures.
 
I will collect and share work, ideas, code, documentation, and other related materials pertaining to Computer Science, ICT, Physics, Mathematics, and Science in general.
